Welcome!

Dr. Subarsha Banerjee is an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at JIS University, Kolkata. He holds a doctorate in Mathematics, with research interests spanning graph theory, combinatorics, and discrete mathematics.

His work focuses on structural graph properties, topological indices, and their interdisciplinary applications, particularly in chemical graph theory and computational modeling.

Dr. Banerjee is actively engaged in both research and teaching, and is committed to guiding students in advanced mathematical thinking and problem-solving.

Research Interests: Graph Theory, Combinatorics, Spectral Graph Theory
